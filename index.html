VQG Vu Quang

Vu Quang is a potential eco-tourism destination. This place has a high biodiversity and often discovers new species of animals. Here, visitors can make adventurous trips, discover the unexpected mysteries of the forest

VQG Pu Mat

Pu Mat National Park has a large area and high biodiversity. With many species of forest animals and plants recently discovered: 2,500 species of plants belonging to 160 families and nearly 1,000 species of animals.

Nguyen Du Relic

Nguyen Du relic area includes a complex of relics of the Nguyen family in Tien Dien commune.This relic complex is a complex of many temples. The cultural heritages in the relic site have historical value still preserved to this day.

A couple of introductions about Ha Tinh

Ha Tinh is a province in the central strip of land,
located in the North Central tourist area,bordering Nghe An
to the north, Quang Binh to the south, Laos to the west,
the East Sea to the east with a 137km long coastline.
The topography is diverse, with enough mountainous, midland,
plain and sea areas. The delta has a small area divided by mountains
and rivers. Ha Tinh has 14 large and small rivers and many lakes.
The province is located in a tropical area, with hot and humid monsoons
and a lot of rain. The average annual temperature is 23.7ÂºC.

Huong Tich Pagoda

Huong Tich Pagoda is classified as one of the 21 landscapes
of the ancient South. The pagoda is located at Huong Tich peak
in Hong Linh mountain range in Thien Loc, Can Loc district, Ha Tinh
with an altitude of 650m above sea level.

Ca Tru

Unlike other traditional folk songs, ca tru is a non-popular art
form and is very "picky" to listeners. Ca Tru also requires
the technique of bouncing seeds, the skillful release of words
of the Ca Nuong and the harmony of the dual lute.

Son Kim hot spring

With a vast area covered with ecological tropical forests
and majestic natural scenery, you will enjoy a vacation
in green space and cool fresh climate.

Nga ba dong loc

Dong Loc junction is a historical relic marking the eternal
youth of 10 brave young girls who volunteered for Dong Loc
and has become a legend in the hearts of the Vietnamese people.

Keo cu do

Cu Do candy is a specialty peanut candy of Ha Tinh province, Vietnam.
Candy is cooked from molasses, sugar, malt, ginger with peanuts added
and poured into two pieces of pressed rice paper. This candy is very sticky
and sticky, can be eaten alone or enjoyed with green tea
